# 易变 Yixchange

> A terminal divination tool based on the ancient I Ching, using the traditional three-coin method.

---

## What is this?

**Yixchange** simulates the [three-coin method](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/I_Ching_divination) of I Ching (易经) divination. You focus on a question, toss three virtual coins six times, and receive a **hexagram (卦)** — a stack of six lines that you can consult for insight and guidance.

Each toss determines one line of the hexagram, and some lines are "changing", meaning they evolve into their opposite — giving you both a present hexagram (**本卦**) and a transformed one (**变卦**).
